Graduate Admissions

Welcome to the graduate admissions office at IUB.

Application Process

Applicants are required to submit the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from all previous institutions
  • Statement of purpose
  • Letters of recommendation
  • GRE or other test scores (if applicable)

Deadlines

Applications for the Fall semester must be submitted by June 1st, and for the Spring semester by November 1st.

Contact Information

For any questions, please contact the Graduate Admissions Office at gradadmissions@iub.edu.bd.
