Scholarships

Supporting your academic journey through financial aid.

Types of Scholarships

  • Merit-based Scholarships: Awarded to students with excellent academic records.
  • Need-based Scholarships: For students demonstrating financial need.
  • Sports Scholarships: For outstanding athletes.
  • Departmental Scholarships: Offered by individual faculties and departments.

How to Apply

Students can apply for scholarships during the admission application process or at the beginning of each academic year.

Contact

For scholarship inquiries, please email scholarships@iub.edu.bd.
