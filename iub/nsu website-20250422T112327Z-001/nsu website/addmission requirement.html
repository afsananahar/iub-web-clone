Admission Requirements

General requirements for undergraduate and graduate admissions at IUB.

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

  • Completion of Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 in HSC or equivalent.
  • Entrance exam results (if applicable).
  • English language proficiency.

Graduate Admission Requirements

  • Completed undergraduate degree from a recognized institution.
  • Minimum CGPA of 3.0 (or equivalent).
  • Letters of recommendation.
  • Statement of purpose.
  • GRE or equivalent test scores (if required).

Additional Requirements

Some departments may have additional requirements or prerequisites. Please check the specific department's guidelines.
