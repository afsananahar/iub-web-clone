Environmental Science

Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science | 120 Credits | 4 Years

Program Overview

The Environmental Science program at IUB trains students to understand and solve environmental challenges through a multidisciplinary approach that includes ecology, chemistry, geography, and policy analysis.
