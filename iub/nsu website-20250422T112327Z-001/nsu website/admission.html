Undergraduate Admissions

Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB)

Eligibility

  • Minimum GPA requirements in SSC & HSC / O & A Levels.
  • Completed application form with all academic records.

Application Process

  • Fill out the online admission form at admissions.iub.edu.bd.
  • Pay the application fee online or at the bank.
  • Upload all required documents.
  • Appear for the admission test or interview (if applicable).

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: July 15, 2025
  • Admission Test: July 22, 2025
  • Orientation: August 10, 2025
